What is Mobile IV Therapy?

Mobile IV therapy is a customizable wellness service that delivers fluids, vitamins, minerals, and medications directly into your bloodstream via an intravenous (IV) drip. Unlike oral supplements, which must pass through the digestive system, IV therapy provides immediate absorption, ensuring maximum effectiveness.
